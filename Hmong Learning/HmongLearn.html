Welcome!

Welcome to our fun and innovative Hmong language class! We are University of Minnesota staff and students, from the Asian Languages and Literature Department, Hmong Program. Our goal is fluency in speaking, reading and writing the Hmong language. We use Hmong history and cultural themes to integrate into our everyday lessons, in order to cultivate and retain the very important aspects of being Hmong. We hope that you have fun looking through out site, our projects and we hope that you are able to utilize some of the free information here for your own learning journey as well. Sib ntsib dua os!

A Whole New View!

What You Will Gain!

Learning another language will introduce you to a new culture. Increased cultural awareness provides for enriched interpersonal exchanges and experiences. You can build relationships with people all over the world. Learn to respect and appreciate different ethnic and cultural values.Enjoy foreign films, music and literature in their original languages. Discover unique histories and traditions.

You might take a second look at your own culture, understanding it in a whole new way.

The Sky is the limit!

What You Will Learn !

  • Beginner Level - Our Intro track is for heritage and non-heritage learners with little to no background in speaking Hmong. These students start from the basic literacy and grammar structures, building foundations in life-long language skills.

  • Intermedete Level - Our regular Beginning and Intermediate Hmong track is for students with a beginning to intermediate level of background in speaking Hmong. This means that students are able to comprehend as well as make basic dialogue at the everyday level.

  • Advance level- Our third track is the Accelerated Beginning and Intermediate track. This track is reserved for students with a higher level of background in speaking Hmong. This track moves at a very fast pace and introduces high level vocabularies as well as intensive reading lessons.
